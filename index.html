Interval Timer Metronome

Android

This is great for any type of interval training, and especially if you want to establish a rhythm or pace, e.g. dance, juggling, or any repetitive activity. This allows you to set up multiple interval timers for whatever timer duration, metronome speed, and metronome time signature you prefer. Optionally you can also disable the metronome all together by setting the beats per minute (BPM) to 0.

VR Dance Trainer

Unity3D, C#, Myo sensors, Occulus Rift

A virtual reality dance studio where your foot steps are tracked by the Myo sensors (using their gyroscope sensors) on each foot. Players are awarded points for demonstrating correct footwork, as well as given visual feedback of their character moving when they correctly step. This game is more of a prototype and was made in 48 hours at a hackathon (Quack Hack 2016)

Pig Dice Game

Android Studio, Java

This is an android game played on a shared screen. It is a game of probabilities where 2 players compete head to head rolling dice to try and reach a certain score first. If you roll a 1 you lose your turn all points accumulated that turn, if you roll any other number add that to your turn score, end your turn whenever you wish by passing the dice instead of rolling. There are many variable options thatn be be used to tweak the rules of the game which lead to different strategies.
